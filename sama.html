Fantasy films are films that belong to the fantasy genre with fantastic themes,
usually magic, supernatural events, mythology, folklore, or exotic fantasy worlds.
The genre is considered a form of speculative fiction alongside science fiction films
and horror films, although the genres do overlap.
Fantasy films often have an element of magic, myth, wonder, escapism, and the extraordinary.
